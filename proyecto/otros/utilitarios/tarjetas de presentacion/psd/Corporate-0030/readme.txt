// COLOR EDITING ////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
=============================================================================

There are 2 ways to edit colors

1) Double click on any vector layers and fill your desired color.

2) Use the Hue/Saturation slider (first layer) to preview or
change the entire design color scheme.
For black and white design, open the Hue/Saturation properties and
tick "Colorize" before editing.

Important Note
--------------
The best method is to use the Hue/Saturation slider for to preview
what color looks best on this design and fill the color manually.
The result is much consistent and more control over the outcome.
